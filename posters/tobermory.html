The Bucket Ballers Collection

By far the most unique ball of ours! Like Margaret, we hadn't purchased him directly, and therefore aren't certain on his make. Unlike Margaret, Tobermory was somehow stuck in a tree before being discovered by Clay. He is one of two rubber balls we own, and likely comes from the 70s. Against all odds, Tobermory is somehow still bowlable.

Tobermory comes from an era of bowling that predates modern bowling ball technology, and therefore doesn't hook the same as younglings nowadays. Although he doesn't see the lanes much anymore, his performance is comparable to that of a slightly-stronger spare ball. Still, we've given him his fair share of 21st-century rev rates and backup balls. In hindsight, we probably made the poor old thing dizzy!
