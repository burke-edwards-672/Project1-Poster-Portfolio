The Bucket Ballers Collection

While not the oldest in our collection, and not the first purchase of ours, this is technically the first ball we had owned! Bestowed upon James by his grandmother, Margaret is James's first choice of spare ball. She can hook if the moment calls for it, but she specializes in straight shots. When James needs a 25mph heater, Margaret is summoned forth.

Because we aren't the original owners, we're not 100% sure on the make. We'd narrowed it down to the Ebonite brand, but Margaret's a mystery beyond that!
