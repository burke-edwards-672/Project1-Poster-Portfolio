The Bucket Ballers Collection

A name of such unholiness belongs to none more deserving. Ryan's Babykiller was the third ball introduced to our arsenal, directly after Scoops and Margaret. Coming from Brunswick, he's often thought of as Scoops's high-octane cousin. Both are advertised as entry-level bowling balls, but the Rhino can turn on a dime like no other. Harder to tame of course, but absolutely exhilarating upon mastery!

Babykiller started out as Ryan's strike ball, but is now used as a spare ball for when straight shots don't cut it. Lightweight and hook-happy, he lends himself to a high-precision style of spare trajectory. BK's slow hook (lovingly called the "BK Special") makes it seem as though he's heat seeking. A corner pin's worst nightmare!
