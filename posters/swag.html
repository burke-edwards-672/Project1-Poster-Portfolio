The Bucket Ballers Collection

Swag was the ball to replace BK as Ryan's main strike ball. While not as sharp with his hooks, he's got the ability to take them farther across the width of the lane. At the time of acquisition, he was the most powerful ball we owned. He now spearheads Ryan's bowling bag with BK as his right-hand man. A two-headed monster, some might say.

Of course, the name and brand were a big factor in our purchase. There's something beautiful about a solid black ball as opposed to the flashy colors of its colleagues. It's the ball a Tom & Jerry gangster would roll. It's even got a dollar sign on it!
