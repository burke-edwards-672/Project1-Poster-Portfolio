The Bucket Ballers Collection

The first bowling ball we'd ever acquired! Scoops is catered towards beginners as an entry-level bowling ball. He hooks more than the house balls, but keeps it pretty mellow overall. For him, consistency is the name of the game. As a result of this philosophy, handling Scoops is an experience once might describe as "controlled". Whatever you think he's gonna do, he'll do it.

Being the oldest has its downsides, unfortunately. Just shy of his third birthday, Scoops developed an equatorial crack - A career-ending injury! Tragic as it may be, he's earned a lavish retirement. Scoops's decorated run includes a summer league, a high game of 266, and a high streak of seven consecutive strikes.
